ABOUT

Basant is the Annual Alumni Reunion of IIT ISM Dhanbad, Where the stars of the show were, at one time, the organizers of the show. The reunion is one of the most emotional times of the ISM calendar especially so for the Alumni of the ages past who come as close to re-living their summer of 69 as possible even if they are literally 69.
