About Us

The Official Formula Racing Team of IIT (ISM)

MECHISMU is a team of Mechanical Engineering students, at Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad which aims to excel in motorsports and spread motorsport passion among Engineering students by participating in racecar design and engineering competitions.
We are a team of individuals from multiple disciplines, driven by the desire of implementing designs-unbounded by the limits of knowledge or technical know-how.
