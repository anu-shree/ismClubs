We The Crew

WTC (We The Crew) is the Street Dance club of IIT(ISM) which was founded on 2nd of December 2014.The founding members of the club is Ajay Meena co-founded by Sanjay Salora and Biswani Kumar Solanki. Club founders had an interest in the uniqueness of street dancing,they saw the emergence and popularity of it,so they started the club to promote the style of street dancing. It was started with 6 members having desire to learn this style of dance expression. WTC mainly focuses on all forms of street dancing.

About Us

The Official Street Dance Club of IIT (ISM)

WTC was founded with a view to promote Street Dancing,since the foundation of the club, it has experienced the difficulties of sustainability of winning as a crew in outer college competitions.

The club takes part in street dance competitions which is actually a more energetic style of dance. Our practice sessions involves focusing on technicalities, power moves and stunts apart from attractive choreographies, and this is what makes it different from other clubs of different colleges.

The practice sessions run throughout the year, every day the practice is of 3 hours and a bit extra on weekends depending upon the upcoming events.

Achievements


# Inter-IIT Cult

The club has taken part in inter IIT cultural meet 2 times, participated in Shuffle Spring fest (cultural festival of IIT KGP) thrice and were 2nd runner-up in 2018-19 and 6th in 2017-18.

updated on 02/02/2019
# College Achievements

Took part in Srijan (cultural fest of IIT(ISM)) and secured 3rd position in Streetbeats (street dance competition) and Hustleherd (group dance competition) in 2017-18 .The main events the club focuses on are INTER IIT Cultural Meet and SPRING FEST.

updated on 31/01/2019
# Randoms

Apart from participating the club organizes different events also such as JAM (just a minute) which is an online solo dancing competition for fresher. It also organizes the event Streetbeats and footloose in Srijan (cultural fest of IIT(ISM)).
